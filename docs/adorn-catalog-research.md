# Adorn catalog research ledger

**Verification date:** 18 July 2026

## Catalog gate

A product enters Adorn only when both checks pass:

1. The brand's own delivery information does not include India.
2. No authorised listing is found at the major Indian beauty channels checked: Nykaa, Tira, and Sephora India.

Grey-market importers, parcel forwarders, marketplace resellers, travel purchases, and second-hand listings do not count as official Indian availability. This is a time-stamped sourcing lead, not a permanent claim. Recheck the official checkout and Indian channels before accepting a customer request.

## Primary availability evidence

- Kulfi Beauty FAQ lists delivery timing only for the US, Canada, and UK: https://kulfibeauty.com/pages/faqs
- Kulfi Beauty store locator surfaces US, Canada, and UK markets: https://kulfibeauty.com/pages/store-locator
- Phytosurgence's official storefront lists shipping for Canada, the US, Hong Kong, Australia, and New Zealand: https://www.phytosurgence.com/
- Cocoacentric states that it does not currently ship internationally: https://www.cocoacentric.com/faq
- Cocoacentric product and price index: https://www.cocoacentric.com/earrings-UfcEP

## Demand and aesthetic evidence

- Indian makeup shoppers explicitly discuss what to ask travellers to bring from the US: https://www.reddit.com/r/IndianMakeupAddicts/comments/15r26y3/relatives_visiting_from_the_us_what_products/
- Community discussion values Phytosurgence's unusual, inclusive shades but also records texture and scarcity concerns; the catalog preserves both sides: https://www.reddit.com/r/OliveMUA/comments/1kjx3sn/falling_out_of_love_with_phytosurgence/
- Pinterest's 2026 makeup direction is used only as aesthetic signal—not product proof—for tactile, eccentric and romantic colour: https://www.pinterest.com/isamaya_beauty/2026-makeup-trends/

## Explicit rejects

Rare Beauty, Fenty Beauty, e.l.f., rom&nd, and Indian accessory brands were excluded because they are officially available in India. Saie was rejected after its official international-shipping page changed to worldwide delivery. Freja New York was rejected because its region selector includes India. Ambiguous brands are rejected rather than labelled unavailable.
