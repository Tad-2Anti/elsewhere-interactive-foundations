# Scent Catalog Research

Verified 18 July 2026. This file is the sourcing ledger for World 02.

## Decision

Scent opens with one coherent, colourful fragrance house: Le Monde Gourmand. A single-brand first edit is more credible than an arbitrary list of viral perfumes because the shipping rule, visual language and price tier remain consistent.

## India availability gate

- The brand's [official FAQ](https://www.gourmandbeauty.com/pages/faq) states that it ships only within the United States.
- No authorised Indian storefront or stockist was found in the audit. Cross-border marketplace pages do not count as Indian authorised distribution.
- Fragrance is regulated dangerous goods. “Sourcing available” is never a promise of ordinary air forwarding; the exact SKU, volume, alcohol classification, carrier and destination rule must pass before quote.

## Quote controls

- Prefer duos, discovery sizes or a single 30 ml bottle for a first request.
- Explain heat exposure and transit risk; do not guarantee an unchanged scent after uncontrolled forwarding.
- Do not make longevity, projection, skin-safety or hypoallergenic claims without product-specific evidence.
- Recheck licensed/limited collections, exact volume, ingredients and carrier acceptance.

## Visual system

Cherry red, banana yellow, coconut cream, tea green and lilac on a plum field. Use translucent liquid shadows, torn foil, fruit cross-sections, soft vapor gradients and bottle-like glass geometry without copying branded bottles or labels.

## Hero concepts — directions only

1. **Gourmand picnic in the clouds** — Oversized banana, coconut shell, tea leaves and a lacquer-red heart float around abstract clear perfume vessels on crumpled pink foil. Everything gathers to the right; the left remains a warm plum haze.
2. **Scent spill scrapbook** — Torn colour swatches look as though fragrance has bled through them. A tilted glass cap, oily light pool and fruit stickers overlap in a youthful, messy composition with one elegant shadow holding it together.
3. **Eight-note vapor totem** — A vertical off-centre stack of translucent colour blocks, each suggesting one scent note. Vapor and glass refraction soften the edges; a yellow ribbon loops through the stack like a smell trail.
