# Content and World Model

## Shared world structure

Every world contains:

- Number and name
- One-line category proposition
- Short description
- Manifesto
- One collage portal image
- Three-color atmospheric palette
- A researched, filterable opening catalog
- Primary sourcing/request action
- Previous and next world links

## 01 Arcade

**Promise:** Official controllers, retro forms and playful hardware beyond India's retail map.

**Feeling:** After-hours play, tactile plastic, electric colour, odd hardware silhouettes.

**Collage vocabulary:** Abstract controller forms, button clusters, cable arcs, a red optical hood, carrying-case texture, paper grid and acrylic shadow.

**Palette:** Violet, cyan, hot pink, acid yellow and near-black.

**Opening catalog:** Eight Nintendo of America products across controllers, hardware and accessories. See `arcade-catalog-research.md` for the shipping evidence, compatibility gates and three hero directions.

## 02 Scent

**Promise:** Playful gourmand fragrance the official store does not ship to India.

**Feeling:** Juicy fruit, translucent glass, sweet colour, vapor and a little theatrical excess.

**Collage vocabulary:** Banana, coconut, tea leaves, glass geometry, liquid shadow, foil, torn scent swatches and vapor.

**Palette:** Cherry, banana yellow, coconut cream, tea green, lilac and plum.

**Opening catalog:** Eight Le Monde Gourmand products across perfume, body oil and discovery sets. See `scent-catalog-research.md` for the official US-only policy and fragrance dangerous-goods controls.

## 03 Carry

**Promise:** One-bag travel systems from a specialist whose official store ships only within the US.

**Feeling:** Departures, modular order, bold colour, zips, tags and movement.

**Collage vocabulary:** Shell corners, straps, packing pods, open cases, route lines, baggage-tag forms and folded colour blocks.

**Palette:** Pool blue, coral, butter yellow, slate and sand.

**Opening catalog:** Eight Tortuga pieces across travel packs, day bags and organisers. See `carry-catalog-research.md` for the US-only shipping evidence, fit controls and hero options.

## 04 Arena

**Promise:** Rare licensed kits, independent footwear and useful game-day gear without an official Indian retail route.

**Feeling:** Court energy, supporter memory, worn mesh, floodlight, bright tape and collector provenance.

**Collage vocabulary:** Cropped jersey panels, basketball outsole, futsal tread, mesh, laces, match tickets, size notes, court arcs and touchline paint.

**Palette:** Acid lime, cobalt, coral, off-white and pitch black.

**Opening catalog:** Nine verified or mandatory-recheck products across SDFC/Eighteen Threads, Mitchell & Ness/Sports Fever, Moolah Kicks, UNITUS, Joma and Preach Athletic Lab. See `arena-catalog-research.md` for sources, confidence levels and the three hero directions.

## 05 Adorn

**Promise:** Makeup and expressive accessories without an official India retail route.

**Feeling:** Play, polish, saturated colour, sculptural punctuation.

**Collage vocabulary:** Smoked plum, kajal black, cream pigment, gold orbit, resin orchid, moving fringe.

**Palette:** Lacquer pink, smoked plum, warm gold, terracotta, ink.

**Opening catalog:** Eight verified products across Kulfi Beauty, Phytosurgence, and Cocoacentric. See `adorn-catalog-research.md` for sources, selection logic, and distribution caveats.

## 06 Little

**Promise:** Open-ended wooden play from a maker that does not ship internationally.

**Feeling:** Built worlds, bright parts, messy stories, natural wood and confident primary colour.

**Collage vocabulary:** Curved track, crane, dollhouse section, wooden figures, spoon, wheel, crayon mark, confetti and mud print.

**Palette:** Sunny yellow, tomato red, cobalt, mint, natural wood and deep violet.

**Opening catalog:** Eight Giant Bean products across railways, dollhouses and pretend play, all age three and up. See `little-catalog-research.md` for safety, compliance, availability and hero directions.

## Copy rules

- Avoid luxury clichés such as “curated excellence” and “timeless elegance.”
- Describe material, use, and feeling concretely.
- Treat sourcing as active discovery; do not imply availability until confirmed.
- Keep homepage descriptions under 90 characters where possible.
- Keep product notes to one sentence.
