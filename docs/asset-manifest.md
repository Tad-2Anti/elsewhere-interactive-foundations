# Asset Manifest

## Production collage system

The visual system deliberately separates the homepage portal from the route hero. Portals are readable at depth and while moving; heroes are closer, more architectural compositions with negative space for route copy. Every production master was generated as original art, inspected, and exported as 1120×1400 desktop and 800×1000 mobile WebP derivatives.

## Derivative targets

For each source image:

- High: 1600×2000, WebP quality 82–88
- Medium: 1200×1500, WebP quality 80–84
- Mobile: 800×1000, WebP quality 76–82
- Optional placeholder: 40×50 blurred WebP or encoded dominant-color block

Do not upscale a generated source smaller than the target. Preserve the 4:5 crop and world focal point.

## Existing SVGs and legacy collages

The older abstract SVGs and Gather/Restore/Roam/Wear/Wonder raster collages remain only as non-primary archive assets. None is referenced by the six-world production gallery or route heroes.

## Audio backlog

Not generated in this milestone:

- One licensed or original ambient loop
- One subtle movement texture
- One portal-focus sound
- One portal-entry sound

Every audio file must include source, creator, license, and permitted-use notes before production use.

## Asset rules

- No copied reference-site imagery or sound.
- No embedded words, logos, watermarks, or recognizable product branding.
- Keep source images outside the public bundle when production derivatives are sufficient.
- Record generation prompts and inspection notes with each source asset.
